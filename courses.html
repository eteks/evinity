DATAWAREHOUSE

An operational database (OLTP) system undergoes frequent changes on a daily basis based on the transactions that take place. The Operational data is updated, added, changed or deleted for every single order that is being processed. Business executive cannot analyze the operational data as the data has undergone a change from time to time and data has been updated due to transactions.Data in the data warehouses (OLAP) system is not as volatile as the data in OLTP system.

The data in the datawarehouse is only added and not deleted or updated. Based on the requirement data set is moved from OLTP to OLAP system at different frequencies. Data is stored in form of Star or Snow flake Schema in form of Dimensions and Facts with required granularity. ETL tools are used to move to data from OLTP system to OLAP system and reporting Tools are used to generate reports from the OLAP system. Reports allowed them to make important corporate decisions based on new perspectives gained from data gathered. The training would be provided for Basic and Advanced level for the expertise in Datawarehouse.
